<objective>
Reset the next-beast prediction to wyvern on two triggers — a fresh login / full relog AND pulling a raid/dungeon boss (ENCOUNTER_START) — while a `/reload` (ReloadUI), zoning, and trash/world-mob combat all preserve the current prediction.

Purpose: Two distinct moments should restart the rotation at wyvern. (1) Fresh login: both `/reload` and a fresh login fire ADDON_LOADED + PLAYER_LOGIN identically, so the reliable discriminator is `PLAYER_ENTERING_WORLD(isInitialLogin, isReloadingUi)` — fresh login → `isInitialLogin == true`; `/reload` → `isReloadingUi == true` and `isInitialLogin == false`; zoning → both false. (2) Boss pull: `ENCOUNTER_START` fires only on raid/dungeon encounter pulls, never on trash or world mobs, so it is the correct trigger (NOT PLAYER_REGEN_DISABLED / generic combat start). Both resets use the identical mechanism: `SetNextBeastId("wyvern")` + `ResetAuraState(false)`. The ADDON_LOADED index-restore stays intact so `/reload` preserves the prediction.

Output: Two new event registrations + two new OnEvent branches (factored through a tiny shared local helper) in PLBeast/PLBeast.lua, updated comments at the ADDON_LOADED index-restore, and reworded TRACK-03 (REQUIREMENTS.md) and Phase 2 success criterion #3 (ROADMAP.md).
</objective>
